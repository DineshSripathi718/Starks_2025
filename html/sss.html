Preperation of potato fry

Description :

Potato Fry is a simple and flavorful Indian side dish made by sautéing diced or sliced potatoes with spices like turmeric, chili powder, mustard seeds, and curry leaves. Crispy on the outside and soft inside, it's a comforting, golden-brown favorite that pairs perfectly with rice or roti.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • oil
  • curry leaves
  • green chillies
  • onion
  • mustard seeds

steps to prepare :

  1. Peel and cut 2–3 potatoes into small cubes.
  2. Add 2–3 tablespoons of oil to a pan and heat it.
  3. Add ½ tsp mustard seeds, a few curry leaves, and 1–2 green chilies
  4. Put the chopped potatoes into the pan and stir well.
  5. Sprinkle salt, ¼ tsp turmeric, and ½ tsp red chili powder
  6. Fry on medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the potatoes are golden and crispy (about 15–20 minutes).
