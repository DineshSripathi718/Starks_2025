Preparation of Chicken Curry

Description

I like chicken curry because it is rich, flavorful, and comforting. It reminds me of home-cooked meals and goes perfectly with rice or roti.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing

Steps to Prepare

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add chopped onions until golden brown
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft
  4. Add turmeric, red chili, coriander powder, and salt Cook the mixture well
  5. Add the chicken pieces and mix well with the spices
  6. Cover and cook on medium flame for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally
  7. Add a little water if needed and simmer until the chicken is fully cooked
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and cook for another 2-3 minutes
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti
