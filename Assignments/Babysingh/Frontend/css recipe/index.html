Preparation Of Stuffed Brinjal Curry

Description:

I had choosen this reciepe because this dish is rich in vitamins,minerals,fiber etc and it is known as king of vegetables.This curry goes perefect with both rice and chappathi.

Ingredients:

  1. Vankaya
  2. Onion-1 large
  3. Oil-3Tbsp
  4. Cumin Seeds-1Tbsp
  5. Salt-2Tbsp
  6. Turmeric powder-1Tbsp
  7. Red Chilli Powder-2Tbsp
  8. Ginger Garlic Paste-1Tbsp
  9. Tamarind-lemon size

Stuffing Masala Ingredients

  1. Peanuts-1cup
  2. coriander Seeds-2Tbsp
  3. Dry Coconut Flakes-2Tbsp
  4. Cinnamon-1stick
  5. Clove-2
  6. Sesame Seeds-1/2cup

Steps To Prepare:

  • Take brinjals to make split on it and soak it for 10mints in salt water
  • Take brinjals which have slits on it and stuff it with the blended masala
  • Take a pan and add some oli
  • Add some clove,sesame,cinnamon and stuffed brinjal to it
  • Cook for 15 mints by stiring it and then add the leftover grinfed masala with some tamarind water
  • As oil is separated from the curry its is well cooked
  • Finally garnish with some coriander leaves
