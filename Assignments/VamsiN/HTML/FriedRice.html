Preparation of Friedrice

Descripion :

Chicken Fried Rice is a flavorful and satisfying dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with tender pieces of chicken, freshÂ vegetables

Ingredients:

  1. boiled rice
  2. eggs
  3. chicken fry pieces
  4. cabbage & carrot
  5. curry leaves and coriander
  6. salt and chilli powder
  7. garam masala and tasting salt
  8. oil

Steps to prepare :

  • take a pan and add some oil
  • Stir the eggs and add to the pan and fry and also add the curry leaves
  • after frying of egg add some chopped green chillis,cabbage and carrot and cook some time
  • now we have to add the fried chicken pieces and add the boiled rice and add the sauces and the masalas
  • and now mix all the ingredients and add the coriander leaves for garnishing
